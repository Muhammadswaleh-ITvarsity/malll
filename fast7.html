FAST 7

Fast and Furious 7 follows Dominic Toretto and his team as they seek revenge against Deckard Shaw, who killed Han and seeks vengeance for his brother Owen. The team, including Brian, Letty, Tej, and Roman,

reunite to help Dom track down Shaw. Along the way, they encounter a terrorist named Jakande, who possesses a powerful surveillance system called God's Eye. The team must rescue Ramsey,

the creator of God's Eye, and prevent Jakande from misusing the technology. The film features high-octane action sequences, including a thrilling airdrop stunt and a final showdown in Los Angeles.

The movie concludes with a poignant tribute to Paul Walker, as Brian O'Conner bids farewell to Dom and drives off into the sunset, symbolizing his retirement from the crew. The film's emotional depth and action-packed sequences make it a memorable installment in the franchise.
