KARATE KID LEGENDS

Karate Kid: Legends follows Li Fong, a kung fu prodigy from Beijing, as he moves to New York City with his mother.

Li struggles to adjust to his new life, complicated by a traumatic past when his brother was killed after a tournament.

He befriends Victor and his daughter Mia, owners of a pizzeria, but faces bullying from Mia's ex-boyfriend Conor, a ruthless karate champion.

Mr. Han and Daniel LaRusso team up to train Li, blending kung fu and karate techniques. Li enters the Five Boroughs Tournament to confront his fears and help Victor financially.

With their guidance, Li masters a hybrid fighting style and faces Conor in the final match. Li wins the tournament using the "dragon kick" and shows mercy by helping Conor up after a cheap shot.

The film concludes with Victor opening a new pizzeria and Li sending a pizza to Daniel as a thank-you gif.
